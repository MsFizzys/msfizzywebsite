Ms. Fizzy's Story

Make your party POPPIN'! Ms. Fizzy's is a mobile beverage cart & catering company in Marin County and the North Bay Area. We're mixing up fun sodas, iced coffees, and more! Never heard of a DIRTY SODA? It's soda with cream and fun flavors!

Available for your next event — birthday party, wedding, corporate gathering, school fundraiser! Ms. Fizzy's is for the whole family. Dirty sodas will soon be your new favorite sweet treat!
